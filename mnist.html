MNIST Handwritten Digit Recognition

PyTorch CNN SGD Momentum Computer Vision

📝 Project Overview

Developed a robust Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to classify handwritten digits from the MNIST dataset. This system achieves high accuracy by learning hierarchical features—from simple edges to complex shapes—directly from raw pixel data.

Dataset: 60,000 Training images (80%) and 10,000 Testing images (approx. 14.3%).

🏗️ CNN Architecture

  • Conv Layers: Four convolutional layers (32 and 64 filters) for deep feature extraction.
  • Spatial Reduction: Max-Pooling layers to reduce dimensionality while preserving important features.
  • Decision Head: Three Fully Connected (Linear) layers with ReLU activation.

⚙️ Training Strategy

  • Optimizer: SGD with a learning rate of 0.025 and 0.09 momentum.
  • Metrics: Monitored Sensitivity and Specificity for each of the 10 digit classes.
  • Loss: Utilized CrossEntropyLoss for multi-class optimization.
